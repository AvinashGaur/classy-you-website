Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply WhatsApp or DM us on Instagram with the product name and your size. We'll confirm availability and share payment details.

What sizes do you carry?

Most styles are available in XS to XL. Some styles also come in numeric denim sizes (26–32). DM us if you're unsure — we're happy to help with measurements.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are processed within 1–4 business days. Delivery across India takes 5–10 business days depending on your location.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Orders above ₹999 qualify for free shipping. For orders below ₹999, a small shipping fee applies based on your location.

Can I return or exchange a product?

All sales are final. We do not accept returns or exchanges. Please review our Refund Policy and reach out before ordering if you have any sizing questions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery for select locations. We'll share details when you place an order.
